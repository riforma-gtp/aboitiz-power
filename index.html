06 — How We Work

Search, structured.

A structured process built for executive search — not volume recruitment. The principal leads every search personally, from brief to placement. There are no handoffs to junior staff at any stage.

01
Brief & Market Map
Detailed intake with the hiring manager and HR. We produce a written brief and a live talent market map before outreach begins — so you understand the depth and geography of the candidate pool before we enter it.
02
International Search
Direct approach to passive candidates — not active job seekers. We reach them through 18+ years of direct relationships built across the energy transition community in APAC, Europe and the Middle East.
03
Structured Assessment
Competency-based interviews, technical evaluation and cultural alignment assessment. We present 3–5 calibrated candidates with a written assessment report per search — not a stack of CVs.
04
Offer & Transition
We support offer negotiation, relocation logistics and counter-offer management. We remain engaged through onboarding and the probationary review — the placement is not complete at the offer letter.
How we reach candidates who are not looking

The profiles AboitizPower needs for offshore wind, LNG, pumped storage and CCGT are not applying for jobs. They are employed, performing well, and rarely visible on job boards. We reach them through a combination of direct outreach backed by name recognition in the community, personal referrals from within active project networks — OEM engineering teams, hydro operators, LNG terminal operators, offshore wind developers — and through relationships built over 18 years of active placement in the energy transition sector. We also draw on SRI Executive's Manila-based research team for local market intelligence and candidate verification. The brief we produce at the start of every search includes a named long-list of target candidates before we make first contact.

"Typical timelines: 6–10 weeks for regional searches. 10–14 weeks for international offshore wind, LNG, pumped storage or specialist thermal profiles."

07 — Engagement Models

Built around your needs.

We offer three engagement structures. We recommend the right model for each search based on seniority, urgency and the nature of the candidate pool. Commercial terms are discussed directly and tailored to the mandate.

01
Retained Search
For SVP / VP / Head-of-function
  • Exclusive — GTP resource fully committed to the mandate
  • Three stages: brief → search → placement
  • Milestone-based fee structure across the search process
  • 12-month replacement guarantee as standard
  • Full written assessment report for each shortlisted candidate
02
Engaged Search
For Director / Senior Manager level
  • Engagement retainer to secure coverage and priority
  • Full international search process and written assessment
  • Success-based fee with retainer offset at placement
  • 6-month replacement guarantee as standard
  • Suitable for multiple concurrent roles at this level
03
Market Intelligence
Talent mapping & workforce planning
  • Compensation benchmarking — regional and international
  • Talent market mapping — where specific profiles exist globally
  • Employer value proposition advice for international hiring
  • Retained on a project or annual advisory basis
  • Particularly relevant for offshore wind and LNG planning
